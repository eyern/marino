Reflection

Hope blooms in the face of adversity

Through pronounced a lost cause, Marino's parents refused to let hope raze away like smoke unfurling from a house in quick scratches on a cold winter day.

Love Conquers all

The Gemma family's distinct love for their little child Marino, fuelled their relentless fight. Their bond, forged in the fires of adversity, transcends the ordinary

Knowledge is power, but curiosity is catalyst

Driven by an insatiable thirst for answers, the family explored every avenue, defying limitations and shattering boundaries. Their journey underscores the crucial role of curiosity in pushing past the horizon of the known and unlocking new possibilities.

Faith shines a guiding light

In the face of the unknown, the Gemma's clung to their faith.

Persistence pays off

Despite the setbacks and pronouncements of futility, the family never surrendered. This ultimately led them to the breakthrough they desperately sought

Every life deserves a fighting chance

Marino's case underscores the inherent value of every human life.

Gratitude unlocks joy

Despite the hardship, the Gemma's never lost sight of Gratitude. Their appreciation for the small victories, the moments of respite that surrounded them reminds us that even in the mdst of trials, a thankful heart can lightthe way towards joy.

Suffering refines resilience

Marino's story is not just of medical triumph but of the human spirit's ability to grow through adversity. His illness forged a family bound by unconditional love, unbounded hope, and an unyielding resolve, leaving behind an imprint of resilience.

Chapter 1

A death knell echoed through the gemma household, leaving parents Mary and Francis clinging to a sliver of hope for their six-month-old son, Marino. The news arrived in a baritone boom, shattering the porcelain peace of the Gemma household like a dropped crystal decanter....

Chapter 2

A young Marino, now a teenager, embarked on a new chapter. The walls of the hospital became his home. Here, he encountered a dedicated Consolata Brother Paul Ferrari who offered him daily religious instruction. Mass and even communion became woven into the fabric of his days, nurturing a burgeoning faith....

Chapter 3

The sun, a molten coin in the twilight sky, was dizzying into a fall, slumbering groggily into the volcanic rim of the Great Rift Valley. While it did so, it cast long shadows accross the dusty "Iringa" expanse. This was the backdrop for a tradition as old as the baobab trees themselves - the sending of the first-year theology students to Tanzania....

Chapter 4

The Nothern Kenyan landscape unfolds like an overexposed photograph, dominated by vivid and intense colour palette Thatreflects the harshness of it's environment. The prevalent hues are bright and blown out, a visual of the relentless sun that governs this region....

Chapter 5

Assuming the role of an assistant Parish Priest for which he had been assigned, Father Marino found himself working with the diocesan priests of Maralal. For an uninterrupted span of four years and a half, he took a deep dive into the demands of this local community , seeking not only to fulfil his ecclesiastical duties but to also cultivate deep and meaningful relationships with people he served....

Chapter 6

Ok, the first feeling was that Father Marino did not believe he had been appointed the Parish Priest of Consolata Shrine. Father Marino's initial reaction to his appointment as Parish Priest of Consolata Shrine was one of disbelief....

Chapter 7

Stepping into the creaky parish office floors of Consolata Shrine felt like Marino had unlocked the doors to a cherished past. It was a homecoming of the soul, one he needed urgently and fervently after time in the Northern frontier of Kenya....

Chapter 8

Back to the cobbled streets of Rome, the familiar swoosh of the Metro and the old, unhurried lifestyle of the Italian population, Fr. Marino found himself boarding to train to Martina Francain Taranto to the familiar shores of his homeland....

Chapter 9

Chapter 10
